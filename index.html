What's this all about?

Irelands's first population genomics study of the lesser trefoil, Trifolium dubium - known to many as the shamrock. Our goal is to collect and sequence samples of T. dubium across Ireland - all whilst spreading the word about the importance and utility of biodiversity genomics!
